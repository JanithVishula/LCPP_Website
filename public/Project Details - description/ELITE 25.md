Project Objective
The objective of the installation ceremony is to formally induct the newly elected office bearers of Leo Club ELITE 25 and recognize the contributions of outgoing members. The event aims to strengthen leadership within the club, motivate members to actively participate in upcoming community service projects, and showcase the club’s commitment to social welfare. Additionally, the ceremony provides an opportunity to engage with the local community, stakeholders, and guests, fostering awareness, support, and collaboration for future initiatives.

Project Need Assesment
Show this section
Benefiting Community	
The primary beneficiaries of the installation ceremony are the members of the Leo Club of Pannipitiya Paradise, the local community, and other stakeholders connected to the club. Club members benefit directly by observing the formal leadership transition, which encourages them to take active roles in upcoming projects. The local community benefits indirectly as the ceremony highlights the club’s commitment to social service and inspires awareness of initiatives that positively impact the area. Guests, family members, and community leaders also gain insight into the club’s structure and programs, strengthening relationships between the club and the wider community.

Identified Community Need	
The community need addressed by the ceremony is a lack of awareness about the Leo Club of Pannipitiya Paradise’s activities and opportunities for youth engagement in social service. Within the club, there is a need to ensure smooth leadership transitions and maintain motivation among members, which is essential for sustaining current and future service projects. The ceremony bridges this gap by formally presenting the new office bearers and highlighting the club’s objectives, while encouraging participation and engagement from both members and the local community.

Service Opportunity	
The installation ceremony provides a key service opportunity by formally installing new office bearers, recognizing outgoing members, and promoting leadership development. It acts as a platform to motivate members to actively engage in upcoming community projects and encourages wider participation from the local community and youth. Hosting the ceremony at Vidma House, Boralesgamuwa from 2:30 PM to 6:00 PM also enhances visibility for the club and fosters collaboration with stakeholders, leading to more effective and impactful community service initiatives throughout the year.

Mode of Data Collection	
Data regarding the effectiveness and impact of the ceremony can be collected through multiple methods. Attendance records and sign-in sheets provide quantitative insight into participation. Feedback forms and informal interviews with members, guests, and stakeholders capture qualitative information about engagement, satisfaction, and suggestions for improvement. Observation during the ceremony and documentation through photographs or videos help assess the overall success of the event and inform planning for future activities.

Project Description
Show this section
The installation ceremony of Leo Club ELITE 25 was held at Vidma House, Boralesgamuwa, from 2:30 PM to 6:00 PM. The event included a welcome speech, speeches from chief guests and dignitaries, the formal installation and oath-taking of new office bearers, and recognition of outgoing members for their contributions. Cultural performances and presentations highlighted the club’s achievements and upcoming service projects. The ceremony also served as a networking platform for members, guests, and community stakeholders, encouraging active participation in the club’s initiatives and reinforcing the values of leadership, service, and unity.
